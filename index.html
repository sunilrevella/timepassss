The Story
Behind the Dum

"I travelled every corner of South India — every city, every dhaba, every street stall — chasing a biriyani that felt like home. I never found it. So I built it."

Our founder, a B.Tech Civil Engineering graduate, spent years working across all of South India — covering every district in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. Every new city was a new adventure, every plate of biriyani a new hope. But time after time, the flavour was different, the consistency lost.

Even back home in Tirupati, the taste changed — every visit brought a different experience. That disappointment sparked a mission: to bring one unwavering, soul-satisfying flavour to every household. A taste that never compromises. A Biriyani Story that never changes its ending.

Today, A New Biriyani Story is Tirupati's cloud kitchen built on that promise — rich masalas, home-cooked warmth, delivered free to your door.
