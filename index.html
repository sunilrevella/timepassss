The Story Behind the Dum

"I travelled every corner of South India — every city, every dhaba, every street stall — chasing a biriyani that felt like home. I never found it. So I built it."

A B.Tech Civil Engineering graduate, our founder spent years working across all of South India — covering every district in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. Every new city, every new plate of biriyani — a new hope. But time after time, the flavour changed, the consistency lost.

Even back home in Tirupati, the taste was different every time. That disappointment sparked a mission: bring one unwavering, soul-satisfying flavour to every doorstep. A New Biriyani Story — where the taste never changes its ending.
